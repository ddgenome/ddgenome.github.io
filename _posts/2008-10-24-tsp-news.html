As discussed in a previous post, the Tumor Sequencing Project has just published its initial findings from sequencing 623 genes in 188 patients with lung adenocarcinoma in Nature, Somatic mutations affect key pathways in lung adenocarcinoma. For a more non-cancer-biologist friendly take on the findings of the paper, you can read the press coverage in the Washington Post, LA Times, Science News, PBS NewsHour, US News & World Report, and Washington University MedNews.
