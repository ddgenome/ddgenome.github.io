For you RSS readers that do not visit the site, I thought I would point out a few of the blogs I have recently added to the Blogroll list on the right-hand side of the page. First, there are the infamous AGBT genomics bloggers, Genetic Future's Daniel MacArthur (the most prolific of the bunch), Mass Genomics' Dan Koboldt, and Anthony Fejes. Others blogging in the genomics field are the imitable Sean Eddy and Steven Salzberg, who recently moved his blog to Field of Science. Finally, there are several more bloggers at The Genome Center, Indraniel Das from our LIMS group and Gary Stiehr, who has blogs on high-performance computing and research IT.
