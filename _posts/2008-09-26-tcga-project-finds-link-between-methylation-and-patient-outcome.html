Researcher from The University of Texas M. D. Anderson Cancer Center recently presented some of their findings from The Cancer Genome Atlas at the American Association for Cancer Research Molecular Diagnostics in Cancer Therapeutic Development. They found that methylation patterns in CpG islands correlate with patient outcomes in glioblastoma. If you are so inclined, you can learn more about CpG islands and methylation.
