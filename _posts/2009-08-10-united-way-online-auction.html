The United Way of Greater St. Louis is having an online auction to benefit many non-profits around St. Louis, including the St. Louis Crisis Nursery. A lot of the items have a local flavor, e.g., sports tickets and landmark tours, but some have a wider appeal like a guitar autographed by Carlos Santana. The auction closes on August 20, 2009, so get your bids in.
