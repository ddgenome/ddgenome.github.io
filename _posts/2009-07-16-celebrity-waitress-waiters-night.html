The St. Louis Crisis Nursery will be hosting their annual Celebrity Waitress and Waiters Night on August 20, 2009 at Canyon Café and Cardwell’s at the Plaza, both located in Plaza Frontenac. St. Louis celebrities and politicians will be waiting on tables and all tips received will be donated to the Crisis Nursery. This is a popular event, so be sure to make your reservations soon.
