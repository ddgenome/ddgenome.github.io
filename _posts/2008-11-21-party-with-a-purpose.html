Last night the St. Louis Crisis Nursery Young Professional Board hosted its first event, Party with a Purpose, at Bar Napoli in Clayton. The evening was a big success: it sold out, everyone had a great time, and, most importantly, we raised a lot of money for the Nursery. Thanks to everyone who came, including Olympic Gold Medal winner Lori Chalupny and St. Louis County Executive Charlie Dooley, and special thanks to our sponsors Stephen and Gail Lambright (Platinum Sponsor), Cafe Napoli (the space and wait staff), Clayton Valet (valet), and Steady Rising Entertainment (DJ). Other sponsors included Pelopidas, St. Louis Auto Dealers Association, Retirement Solutions, LLC, St. Louis Magazine, Summit Signs & Graphics, Saks Fifth Avenue, and Saville Row Custom Clothier.
